SoundChanger (forked from Mark Rosenfelder's SCA²)
[?]
  • This is a simple REGEX script that allows to check how good a given set of sound change rules converts a given ancestor language into its descendant language.
  • As a default, 517 word pairs from Old High German and modern Standard German are supplied for testing.
  • You can, however, easily replace the data with your own pair of ancestor and descendant language.
  • The rules should be straightforward, since I tried to design them in such a way that they look almost identical with rules in historical linguistics.
  • For details, you can further check this document (originally a part of a lecture) in which some more explanations are given (in German).
